## 👩‍💻 About Me

I am a final-year Computer Science student at **Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore**, specialising in Artificial Intelligence. I graduate in **June 2026** and am a Singapore Permanent Resident.

I work across the full path from ambiguous problem to tested system — framing the need, designing the technical approach, implementing the model or product, evaluating it honestly, and communicating the result with evidence.

My strongest work sits at the intersection of:

- 🤖 **Machine Learning & Deep Learning** — PyTorch, transformers, GNNs, multimodal fusion
- 👁 **Computer Vision & Multimodal Systems** — ConvNeXt, ViT, CLIP, multi-task learning
- 🧬 **Research Engineering** — protein language models, graph-based structure learning, leakage-aware evaluation
- ⚡ **Generative AI & LLMs** — local LLM deployment, RAG, multi-agent workflows, on-device inference
- 🏗 **Full-Stack Products** — React, Node.js, MongoDB, Redis, REST APIs, CI/CD
- 📊 **Data Science & Analytics** — feature engineering, ensembling, dashboards, ETL

> **Build carefully. Evaluate honestly. Explain the trade-offs.**
